Calculate your HEXACO score and MBTI type!!


HEXACO score defines the personality of a person in numerical values based on the following six factors: Honesty-Humility (H), Emotionality (E), Extraversion (X), Agreeableness (A), Conscientiousness (C), and Openness to Experience (O).


MBTI test attempts to assign a value to each of four categories: Introversion (I) or Extraversion (E), Sensing (S) or Intuition (N), Thinking (T) or Feeling (F), and Judging (J) or Perceiving (P).
